## Advanced Learning Path: Humanoid AI Systems Development

Welcome to the advanced learning path! This path is designed for experienced robotics engineers, researchers, or PhD students who want to develop cutting-edge humanoid AI systems. The curriculum focuses on research-level concepts, advanced implementations, and state-of-the-art techniques in humanoid robotics.

### Prerequisites
- Strong programming skills (Python/C++/CUDA)
- Advanced mathematics background (linear algebra, calculus, probability)
- Significant robotics experience (multiple projects)
- Understanding of machine learning and deep learning

### Expected Duration
10-12 weeks (5-6 hours per week)

### Learning Outcomes
By completing this path, you will:
- Implement state-of-the-art AI algorithms for humanoid robots
- Design and conduct robotics research projects
- Deploy advanced systems on real hardware
- Contribute to the robotics research community
- Lead complex humanoid robotics initiatives
- Understand and contribute to robotics ethics and societal impact

### Certificate Requirements
To earn your certificate for the advanced path, you must:
- Complete all weekly modules (pass rate: 80% or higher)
- Submit all research-level projects
- Participate in at least 15 research discussions
- Complete the final research project with publication-ready quality
- Present your research to the community
